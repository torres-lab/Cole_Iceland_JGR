bsltRhyCulled.mat = basalt and rhyolite samples from GEOROC with outliers removed. Columns are measurements of Na, K, Ca, Mg, and Si in units of mol percent.
Iceland_CompiledWaterData.xlsx = all of the compiled hydrochemistry data. Units and variables described in the file.
MoultonClayDataMolPercent.xslx = solid phase data for clay size separates from Moulton et al. Units are mol percent.
thorpeClayData.xlsx = solid phase data for clay size separates from Thorpe et al. Data are in weight percent oxide.
